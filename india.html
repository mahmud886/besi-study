Fee Structure for Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh and NRI's from GCC countries:

Fee structure of all programmes (excluding PhD programmes & MBBS) for students from Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh is same as the fee structure for Indian students. Course fee for Bangladeshi student will be 80-90 thousand INR (equivalents to around BDT 1 lac) per year.

Guidelines for Admissions

  • - For foreign nationals, the pre-qualification for applying to all Undergraduate programmes is submission of a certificate equivalent to Class 12th examination of the country and meeting the equivalence criteria of 'Association of Indian University'.
  • - Applicants who are currently or have appeared for their final year graduation examinations in the year 2020 may also apply for admission to Post Graduate programme.
  • - To qualify for admission in Sharda University in any Undergraduate programme, the applicants must complete 17 years of age on 31st December of the academic year in which they are seeking admission.

Scholarship:

The University provides merit based scholarships and continuation of scholarship is as per the terms and conditions of the University i.e. regularity in attendance, progress, good character and conduct. To check your eligibility, please complete the 2nd step of Application form and attach your transcripts.

Early-bird Scholarship:

Register and avail 20% scholarship in Degree Programmes & upto 50% scholarship in M.Tech/Ph.D/MCA Programmes.
