Book my IELTS test

BESI IELTS Bangladesh delivers the IELTS test four times a month or 48 times in a year.

How to book your IELTS test?

You may either book YOUR IELTS test online or come to your nearest IDP branch to book it test offline. Here are the steps:

  • Pick your desired test location/city from the tables below.
  • Choose your test type – Academic/General Training.
  • Find a date that suits you the best.
  • Select the option ‘Book Test.’
  • Fill up the required details and complete the online application.
  • Proceed to pay the test fee.

P.S: Be extremely careful while choosing the module you wish to take.

How much does the IELTS test cost?

  • The IELTS test fee is BDT 17,500
  • The IELTS for UKVI test fee is BDT 19,700
  • The IELTS Life Skills test fee is BDT 13,100

Fee payment

You will need a credit/debit card (VISA / MasterCard) to make a payment online or pay by cash at your nearest IDP Bangladesh office- Gulshan, Dhanmondi or Chittagong. You can also register and pay the test fee at any of our registration partners across the country, closest to your convenient location. To find the nearest registration partner, call us anytime at 09666782782. You will also have to upload a clear, scanned colour copy (less than 1.5MB size) of your passport to complete the process.

Booking confirmation

Make sure that you complete your payment within two days, else the booking will get deferred. Once the payment is received, you will receive an email receipt confirming it. You will also receive a final confirmation detailing you on your test date, reporting time, venue, test schedule and candidate number five to seven days before your IELTS test.

For your Speaking Test, the details will be emailed two days before your Speaking test date. Please note that your Speaking test may be held either before or after your Listening, Reading, and Writing test, depending on your location. Ensure that your email account settings allows IDP emails and keep checking your spam/junk folder in case you don’t seem to receive any.
