About Us

Welcome to BESI Study Consultancy

BESI Study Consultancy is a higher education admission consultancy firm, which is a sister concern of BESI (Bangladesh Engineering and Safety Institute) Private Limited. Our aim is to develop an accessible platform for admission guidance for students aspiring to secure an admission in leading universities around the world. To achieve this aim, we work with multiple higher education institutions in Canada, India, and the United Kingdom.

Our experienced consultants are aware of university admission policies, rules, and regulations, which enable them to provide informative, practical, and impartial advice and guidance on numerous courses that prospective students can choose from.

We have a highly efficient team that provides fast response to prospective student enquiries. Our qualified team of consultants can instantly offer a wide range of solutions to student enquiries either for admission guidance, scholarship information and support, or offering generic information in order for students to make up their mind about pursuing higher education on a particular subject and university.

Our Mission

BESI Study Consultancy’s mission is to close the growing guidance gap for Bangladeshi students in leading international universities’ admission through providing proper guidance, accurate information, advice, and support to prospective students seeking university admission.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading admission consultancy firm where students can access for accurate information, guidance, advice, and support for higher education admissions.
